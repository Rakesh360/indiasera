About US

Who We Are

India's Era is an independent research organisation founded by Banaras Hindu University Students, Aluminis and Professor to work not only as a Think Tank on History, Culture, Economics, Politics, Society and Environment but also to provide a platform for academic guidance and growth..

Our Vision & Mission

It is a family of professors, researchers, scholars, professionals and experts working unitedly, with a commitment of making India's Era substantial and potential voice of India through its discrete initiatives, quality research, fact analysis, environmental studies, social studies, career and intellectual events in educational institutions across the country..
